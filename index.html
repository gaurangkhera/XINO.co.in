XINO

Extreme Innovation.

XINO, the computer club of DPS Rohini comprises of die hard fanatics of technology whose passion for the technological science verges on borderline crazy. We comprise of 25+ brilliant minds, each posessing a unique perspective of analysing the world around us.

"When all think alike, then no one is thinking." We value different ideas, dreams and aspirations of our members and work our best to turn those dreams into reality. "It isn't the incompetent who destroy an organization. The incompetent never get in a position to destroy it. It is those who achieved something and want to rest upon their achievements who are forever clogging things up". We appreciate our achievements but never stop our crusade to acheive long lasting glory in the field of technological sciences. We have taken the competition scene in Delhi by storm, winning innummerable events but never letting it get in our way of learning.

The Events

The crux of XINO

Exquisite - Hackathon

An event for those who wish to be frontrunners in this generation of technology. The preliminary submissions will be judged based on the quality of the product and its functionality. The Top 6 teams will pitch their ideas to our judges.

Tech Exult - Quiz

We welcome the ones who have a knack to keep up with trivia in the world of technology. May the best quizzer win. Top six teams that qualify the prelims will battle it out in a Zoom meeting. May the best quizzer win.

X-Word - Crossword

"Think out of the box but inside the grid." The prelim will be held using a custom-developed platform. The top six teams that qualify will battle it out in a Zoom meeting.
